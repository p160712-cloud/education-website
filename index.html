Introduction

Information and Communication Technology (ICT) refers to digital technologies used to access, manage, and share information. In modern education, ICT has become an important tool for improving teaching and learning processes.

ICT allows teachers to present lessons in more interactive ways and helps students access learning materials anytime and anywhere. The integration of ICT in education supports innovative teaching methods and enhances learning engagement.

ICT Tools in Education

  • Learning Management Systems (LMS) such as Moodle and Google Classroom
  • Online learning platforms like Zoom and Microsoft Teams
  • Educational mobile applications
  • Artificial Intelligence learning systems
  • Digital multimedia learning resources

Benefits of ICT in Education

  • Improves student engagement and motivation
  • Provides flexible learning opportunities
  • Allows access to global learning resources
  • Supports collaboration between students and teachers
  • Enhances digital literacy skills

Challenges of ICT in Education

  • Digital divide between students
  • Lack of technical skills among teachers and students
  • Internet connectivity problems
  • High cost of technological infrastructure
  • Need for proper training and support
