Introduction

Information and Communication Technology (ICT) refers to technologies that provide access to information through telecommunications. ICT plays an important role in modern education by improving teaching methods and learning experiences.

ICT Tools in Education

  • Learning Management Systems (LMS)
  • Online learning platforms such as Zoom and Microsoft Teams
  • Educational mobile applications
  • Artificial Intelligence learning systems

Benefits of ICT in Education

  • Improves student engagement
  • Provides flexible learning opportunities
  • Gives access to global learning resources
  • Encourages collaboration between students and teachers

Challenges of ICT in Education

  • Digital divide between students
  • Lack of technical skills
  • Internet connectivity issues
  • High cost of technology implementation
