With the rapid development of information technology, educational technology plays an increasingly important role in modern education. It has not only changed traditional teaching methods but also provided learners with a more flexible and diverse learning environment.

Through the Internet, multimedia technology, and online learning platforms, students can access learning resources anytime and learn at their own pace. Meanwhile, teachers can use digital tools to design more vivid and interactive classroom activities.
